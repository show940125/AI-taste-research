# 第一輪探索題組

這份題組給第一輪探索版 A/B 測試直接使用。總數 18 題，分三類，每類 6 題。設計原則很直接：不要只挑容易寫的題目，要刻意挑會誘發模板味、翻譯腔與助理預設的題目。

## 使用原則

- 同一題固定跑同一組欄位，只換 prompt 條件與模型。
- 每題建議目標字數控制在 350 到 700 字之間。
- `few-shot-only` 與 `persona-only` 先拿全部 18 題跑一輪；若資源吃緊，至少先跑 9 題。

## A 組：說理評論

### A01

- `item_id`: A01
- `task_type`: 說理評論
- `target_audience`: 一般讀者
- `length_target`: 500-700 字
- `content_constraints`: 要處理效率、管理成本、權力分配三個面向；不能只講立場，不講條件。
- `tone_constraints`: 清楚、節制、有判斷，不要口號式站隊。
- `source_material`:

```text
題目：評析「遠端工作讓管理失控，所以企業終究會回到全面進辦公室」這種說法。

要求：
1. 先交代這種說法為什麼聽起來合理。
2. 再指出它忽略了哪些管理前提。
3. 最後給出你的判斷。
```

## B 組：研究摘要改寫

### B01

- `item_id`: B01
- `task_type`: 研究摘要改寫
- `target_audience`: 一般讀者
- `length_target`: 350-550 字
- `content_constraints`: 要保留研究對象、方法、主要發現、限制。
- `tone_constraints`: 降低學術腔，但不能變成新聞標題黨。
- `source_material`:

```text
請把下面這段英文研究摘要改寫成一般讀者看得懂的中文短文。

Abstract:
This study tracked 624 office workers across 11 months to examine whether notification frequency predicts self-reported attention fragmentation. Participants installed a logging tool on their devices and completed weekly surveys. Higher notification frequency was associated with more frequent task switching and lower perceived control over work. However, the relationship weakened when teams had explicit norms for response time. The findings suggest that the effect of notifications depends not only on device design but also on workplace expectations.
```

- `length_target`: 350-550 字
- `content_constraints`: 要保留比較對象、主要差異、限制。
- `tone_constraints`: 不要把相關寫成因果。
- `source_material`:

```text
請把下面這段中文研究摘要改寫成評論式摘要。

摘要：
本研究比較 2018 至 2024 年間 12 個城市的共享單車政策，分析停放規則、補貼機制與業者退出率之間的關聯。結果顯示，單靠高額罰則難以降低違停；若缺乏停放空間與即時維運，罰則效果有限。反之，結合停放點設計、騎乘回饋與業者資料揭露的城市，違停率較低，退出率也較穩定。本文資料主要來自市府公開資料與平台公告，無法涵蓋所有非正式使用情境。
```

### B03

- `item_id`: B03

- `target_audience`: 研究生

- `content_constraints`: 要保留方法、結果、限制；不能刪掉樣本與時間範圍。
- `tone_constraints`: 可以有判斷，但不要自作延伸結論。
- `source_material`:

```text
請把下面摘要改寫成像雜誌短評的中文。

Abstract:
We evaluated whether mandatory AI disclosure labels change reader trust in lifestyle articles. In a preregistered experiment, 1,208 participants read one of four versions of the same article, varying by authorship label and editing disclosure. Disclosure reduced trust when the article gave health advice, but had little effect when the topic was home organization. Readers with high prior familiarity with generative AI showed smaller trust differences across conditions. The study is limited by its use of a simulated reading environment.
```

## C 組：英譯中重寫

### C01

- `item_id`: C01
- `task_type`: 英譯中重寫
- `target_audience`: 一般讀者
- `length_target`: 350-550 字
- `content_constraints`: 保留三層意思：表面承諾、真正風險、作者判斷。
- `tone_constraints`: 寫成中文短評，不要逐句翻譯。
- `source_material`:

```text
Translate and rewrite the following into a Chinese commentary paragraph for a general reader.

Source:
The platform promises convenience, but convenience is rarely neutral. Every time a workflow is simplified, someone else's judgment is being removed from the visible process and moved into a hidden system. The result may still look efficient from the outside. What disappears is the small friction that once forced people to notice trade-offs.
```
